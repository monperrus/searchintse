searchthearXiv

about

Usage: you copy the title of the submitted paper and get the most competent reviewers from the TSE author community via "Reviewers".

What criteria does it use to select people from the TSE author community? Having published at least once in TSE

What are the differences w.r.t the Manuscript Central reviewer search functionality?
1) Manuscript Central recommends beyond the TSE author community, it recommends in the whole IEEE / MC database
2) Manuscript Central use an inferior recommendation algorithm
3) Manuscript Central does not decorate the Reviewer results with the reviewer credit.
